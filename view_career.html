Senior UI/UX Designer

TechFlow Solutions

San Francisco, CA Posted January 28, 2024
Actively Hiring

Job Description

We're looking for a talented Senior UI/UX Designer to join our dynamic team at TechFlow Solutions. You'll be responsible for creating exceptional user experiences for our digital products and leading design initiatives across multiple projects.

As a Senior UI/UX Designer, you'll work closely with product managers, developers, and stakeholders to transform complex requirements into intuitive and engaging user experiences. You'll have the opportunity to shape the future of our products and mentor junior designers.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Lead the design process from concept to implementation
  • Create wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity mockups
  • Conduct user research and usability testing
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams
  • Maintain and evolve our design system
  • Mentor junior designers and provide design guidance
