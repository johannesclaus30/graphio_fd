About Alexandra

Welcome to my creative space! I'm Alexandra, a passionate UI/UX designer and brand specialist with over 8 years of experience creating digital experiences that connect with users and drive business results.

My design philosophy centers around the belief that great design should be both beautiful and functional. I specialize in creating intuitive user interfaces, compelling brand identities, and comprehensive design systems that help businesses tell their story effectively.

When I'm not designing, you can find me exploring San Francisco's vibrant art scene, trying new coffee shops, or experimenting with photography. I believe inspiration comes from everywhere, and I love incorporating diverse influences into my work.
